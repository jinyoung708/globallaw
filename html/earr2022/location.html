LOCATION

  • How to arrive (Transportations)

  • BY EXPRESS SUBWAY

    Take the Express Subway from Incheon International Airport Station(인천국제공항) to Seoul Station(서울역). -Approximately 1 hour.
    After, catch a taxi in front of the Seoul Tran Station, or take bus (No.501) to Soongsil University.

  • BY CALL VAN SERVICE

    It is much more convenient when moving as a group. The Jumbo Taxi can hold up to 5 passengers and the fare is a flat rate not based on per person.
    It will take approximately 90-95 minutes under normal traffic conditions. (Taxi stop at Incheon Airport / Fare: 90,000 KRW)

  • TAXI

    Once you clear customs, and exit the main terminal, go to taxi stop No.5C where there are taxies that will go to Seoul.
    (Fare : approximately 70,000 KRW)
